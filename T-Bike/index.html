How to T-Bike

Download T-Bike app

Download the T-Bike app from the
Appstore or the Playstore

Locate a T-Bike

Use the T-Bike app to find the closest vehicle to you or look out for a T-Bike Zone around you.

Scan QR code

To unlock the vehicle, simply scan the QR code located on the panel.

Ride Safely

Enjoy the T-Bike ride but remember to abide by all the traffic laws and parking policies.

End your ride

To end your ride, park the vehicle at a T-Bike Zone, lock it and click on the End button on your app.
